Data Deletion Instructions

App Name: Neon Loop

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Neon Loop stores all data locally on your device. No personal data is stored on any external server. You can delete all app data using any of the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Reset

1. Open Neon Loop

2. Tap the ⚙ (Settings) icon in the top-right corner

3. Tap "RESET PROGRESS"

4. Your game progress, star ratings, and preferences will be deleted immediately.

Method 2: Uninstall the App

1. Go to your device's Settings → Apps → Neon Loop

2. Tap "Uninstall"

All locally stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

Method 3: Clear App Data

1. Go to your device's Settings → Apps → Neon Loop

2. Tap "Storage"

3. Tap "Clear Data"

This will remove all saved game data without uninstalling the app.

What Data Is Stored?

  • Game progress (current level)
  • Star ratings per level
  • Sound and haptic preferences

All of this data is stored only on your device using AsyncStorage. No data is transmitted to external servers.
