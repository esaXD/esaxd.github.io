Data Deletion Request

At PawDream AI, we respect your privacy and provide a simple way to request the deletion of any data associated with your use of our application.

How to request data deletion:

Since PawDream AI does not use account-based registration, most of your data (like preferences and local history) is stored locally on your device. You can clear this data by uninstalling the application or clearing the app cache in your device settings.

If you have any concerns regarding data that might have been processed during AI generation, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS HERE]
  2. Subject: Data Deletion Request - PawDream AI
  3. In the email, please specify if you would like us to ensure any transient data related to your sessions is purged.

What data will be deleted?

Upon request, we will ensure that any temporary data related to your AI generation sessions (which are typically not stored permanently) are completely removed from our processing logs. Please note that data stored locally on your phone can only be deleted by you (by uninstalling the app).
