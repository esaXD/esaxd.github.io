Data Deletion Instructions

App Name: Main Character

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Main Character stores all data locally on your device. We do not store any of your data on external servers. You can delete your data using any of the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion

Open Main Character, go to Settings, and tap Clear History. This will delete all your generated narratives and scene history.

Method 2: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Main Character from your device will permanently remove all app data, including:

  • Your name and genre preferences
  • Your set home location
  • All generated narrative history
  • All app settings

What Data Is Deleted

Since all data is stored locally on your device, deletion is immediate and complete. There is no server-side data to request deletion of. Specifically, the following is removed:

  • User preferences (name, selected genre, home location)
  • Narrative history (all generated scenes and chapters)
  • Onboarding status

Third-Party Data

Main Character uses OpenStreetMap Nominatim and Open-Meteo for location names and weather data. These services process requests in real-time and do not store user-specific data on our behalf. For their data practices, please refer to their respective privacy policies.
