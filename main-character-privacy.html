Privacy Policy

App Name: Main Character

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

At Main Character ("we", "us", or "our"), we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and shared when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

1.1. Location Data

Main Character uses your device's GPS location to generate personalized narrative content about your day. Location data is used to:

  • Determine your current city or area name (via reverse geocoding through OpenStreetMap Nominatim)
  • Estimate your activity (walking, driving, stationary) based on movement speed
  • Detect whether you are near your set home location

Location data is processed in real-time and stored only on your device. We do not transmit your GPS coordinates to our own servers.

1.2. Weather Data

The app sends your approximate coordinates to the Open-Meteo API (a free, open-source weather service) to retrieve current weather conditions. Open-Meteo does not require an API key and does not track users. For more details, see Open-Meteo's Terms.

1.3. On-Device Storage

Your name, genre preferences, home location, and generated narrative history are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server owned by us.

1.4. No Account Required

Main Character does not require account creation, login, email, or any personally identifiable information beyond the first name you optionally provide within the app.

2. Third-Party Services

2.1. OpenStreetMap Nominatim

We use the Nominatim geocoding service to convert GPS coordinates into a human-readable city or area name. This request includes your latitude and longitude. For more information, see OpenStreetMap Foundation's Privacy Policy.

2.2. Open-Meteo

We use the Open-Meteo API to retrieve weather data based on your coordinates. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source service. See Open-Meteo Terms.

2.3. Expo / React Native

The app is built using the Expo framework. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics to improve their services. For more details, see Expo's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties. The only external services that receive limited data are OpenStreetMap Nominatim and Open-Meteo, solely for the purpose of providing location names and weather information.

4. Data Retention

All narrative history and user preferences are stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear History" option within the app's Settings screen
  • Uninstalling the application

5. Children's Privacy

Main Character is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at otagdigital@gmail.com.
