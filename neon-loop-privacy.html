Privacy Policy

App Name: Neon Loop

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

At Neon Loop ("we", "us", or "our"), we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and shared when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

1.1. On-Device Storage

Our application uses AsyncStorage to store your game progress, level completion data, star ratings, and user preferences (sound and haptic settings) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

1.2. No Personal Data Collection

Neon Loop does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, login, or any personal information to use the app.

2. Third-Party Services

2.1. Advertising

Our application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

2.2. Expo / React Native

The app is built using the Expo framework. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics to improve their services. For more details, see Expo's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. The only third-party service that may access limited device data is Google AdMob for the purpose of serving advertisements.

4. Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application or using the "Reset Progress" option within the app settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Neon Loop is a casual puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since the app does not collect any personal information, it complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at esaxdeveloper@gmail.com.
